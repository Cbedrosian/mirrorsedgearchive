Welcome to our archive. We currently have about 20 gigabytes of photos, videos, music, art, developer presentations and other materials available. All of this content is hosted on a server, for which we pay the bills. We can only keep this project up and running if people don't abuse this service. This means:

  • Do not use scripts to batch download the entire archive, instead contact us and we can arrange a convenient transfer of the whole archive.
  • You are free to share the contents of this archive, but we ask you to refrain from embedding them into other sites, as this generates a lot of expensive traffic. If you want to use any of it for your own project, download the content, reupload it to your own site or use a free hosting service such as pomfe.co.
  • If you use content from this archive, please note that most of it is not public domain, let alone released for modification. We know that we are in a legally diffused area because we provide content that is subject to the copyright of others, but we hope that our non-profit approach will be respected by the copyright owners.

tl;dr: Use this service at your discretion, but don't abuse it.
