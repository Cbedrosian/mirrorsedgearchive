Project Propaganda | WORK IN PROGRESS


A gallery created by /u/mirrorsedgefan, /u/YoungNAF and /u/xtramaniagamer.

This game includes a lot of interesting video messages and news broadcasts from Gabriel Kruger (CEO of KrugerSec), Aurore Lupain-Tanner (newscaster for Glass Daily News) and Rebecca Thane (Leader of Black November), establishing the lore in much more detail than the average player would ever notice.

The footage below was screen-recorded in 1080p with 60 FPS in the best quality available. It turned out, that extracting the video is a rather complicated task that we couldn't accomplish, due to the nature of how video files are stored in the Frostbite engine. However, thanks to Matti Hietanen's camera tool, we were able to create an almost borderless recording of all videos.
