Formal Takedown request

⚠ Misuse of this process may result in legal consequences! ⚠

This notification is initiating a legal process - please be careful to be accurate in your copyright infringement claims. You may wish to first consult with an attorney to determine whether the content you are reporting is in fact infringing and not protected by fair use, license, or other legal basis. Please note that Takedown Notices received through any other means of contact will not be processed! To submit a valid takedown notice, state the following information:

I am,

  • The owner of the infringing content.
  • an agent, commissioned and allowed to file this takedown notice on behalf of the rightholder.
    • Rightsholder Full Legal Name
    • Rightsholder Country
  • Your Full Legal Name
  • Your Company/Organisation
  • Your Job Title / Position
  • Your Email
  • Your Address
  • Country
  • Your ZIP/Postal Code
  • Your City
  • Content type, such as
    • Audio
    • Video
    • Graphics
  • URL of infringing content
Include this at the end of your message:

I am the owner, or an agent authorized to act on behalf of the owner of an exclusive right that is allegedly infringed.

legal consequences for making false or bad faith allegations of copyright infringement by using this process.
