Mirror's Edge™ Catalyst Closed Beta access details

The players we select to take part will receive an email invite to play the Mirror’s Edge Catalyst Closed Beta.

Sign up on mirrorsedge.com

  1. Visit mirrorsedge.com.
  2. Click BETA in the navigation bar.
  3. Click REGISTER NOW.
  4. Select the platform you prefer from Origin PC, Xbox One, and PlayStation 4.
    • If you choose PlayStation 4, you'll also need to select the country that you registered your PSN Account in.
  5. Click CONTINUE to complete your registration.

When you finish signing up, you'll be given the option to subscribe to the EA Newsletter to receive the latest news on Mirror’s Edge Catalyst, as well as other EA games, services, and events. 

Sign up for PC through our Game Tester Program 

  1. Visit ea.com and click Log In.
  2. Sign in to your EA Account, then click your username followed by My Account.
  3. Click the Game Tester Program tab.
  4. Check the Mirror's Edge box, along with boxes for any other games you're interested in.
  5. Click Update.

You can increase your visibility for future event emails by checking the Contact Preferences under Privacy Settings in My Account on origin.com and selecting the first box.

Stay tuned to mirrorsedge.com, Twitter, and Facebook for Mirror’s Edge Catalyst updates, including when the demo drops.

IMPORTANT NOTES: 

  • We will allow live streaming and image capturing of gameplay for this Closed Beta. Feel free to share videos and impressions online. 
  • All communication is sent from noreply@ea.com, so add this address as a safe sender to receive communications in your inbox. Check your spam, junk, or social folder for any missed email messages from noreply@ea.com.
