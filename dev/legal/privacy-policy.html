Cookies

However, the Internet pages of the The Mirror's Edge Archive use cookies. Cookies are text files that are stored in your web browser.

We use these cookies exclusively to skip the repeated playback of our intro animation on the homepage, and to hide references to this privacy policy after you've seen the notice serveral times, or manually closed it. Additionaly our Content-Delivery-Network, Cloudflare, uses a cookie to identify that your browser is trustworthy. This cookie does not correspond to any user ID, and does not store any personally identifiable information.

If you wish to prevent us from using these cookies, you can revoke the respective permission for our web page through the settings of your web browser at any time. Please note that this might compromise your user experience.
